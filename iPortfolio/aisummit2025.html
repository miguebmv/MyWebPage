RVATech Data + AI Summit, Richmond

[Richmond Virginia Technology Conference]

In March 19, 2025, I had the privilege of participating in the Richmond Virginia Technology (RVATech) Data + AI Summit, one of the region’s premier events dedicated to the latest trends in data science, artificial intelligence, and digital transformation. The summit brought together hundreds of professionals, thought leaders, and innovators to explore how organizations are navigating the evolving AI landscape.
As part of Premier Analytics Consulting, Ryan Paul Lafler and I delivered a 50-minute presentation titled “Building Better Data Science Workflows”. Our session focused on practical strategies with Git, GitHub, and DVC for collaborative data science, reproducible machine learning pipelines, and effective software versioning. With more than 110 professionals in attendance, ranging from data scientists to DevOps engineers and AI practitioners, the talk highlighted the importance of robust workflows in ensuring scalability, transparency, and long-term success in data-driven projects.
Beyond our presentation, the summit was a unique opportunity to engage with leaders at the forefront of AI innovation, exchange best practices, and learn how enterprises are integrating open-source and cloud-native tools into their analytics ecosystems. The experience not only reinforced the growing demand for workflow-oriented approaches in data science, but also showcased the impact of cross-disciplinary collaboration in shaping the future of AI and analytics.
