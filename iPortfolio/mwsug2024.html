MWSUG 2024 Conference     Waukesha, Wisconsin

[Midwestern SAS Software Users Group]

In October 2024, I had the privilege of attending the Midwestern SAS Users Group MWSUG Conference in Waukesha, Wisconsin — a vibrant gathering of data scientists, statisticians, and analytics professionals from across the region. The event highlighted innovative applications of analytics in industry and academia, with a strong focus on both traditional SAS tools and modern integrations with open-source technologies.
Together with Ryan Paul Lafler [Premier Analytics], I co-authored and presented a paper on the integration of Python’s libraries for data cleaning, processing, and modeling within the SAS Viya framework. Our work showcased how Python’s flexibility and SAS Viya’s enterprise-scale capabilities can be seamlessly combined to build reproducible, efficient, and production-ready machine learning pipelines. The presentation sparked insightful conversations on hybrid workflows, reproducibility in analytics, and the growing role of open-source ecosystems within SAS environments.
The conference also offered a fantastic platform to network with industry leaders, exchange practical techniques, and explore real-world case studies. Beyond the technical sessions, MWSUG fostered a collaborative spirit where new ideas, coding strategies, and best practices were shared openly — reinforcing the importance of bridging proprietary and open-source technologies in today’s data-driven world.
