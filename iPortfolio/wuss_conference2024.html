WUSS 2024 Conference, Sacramento

[Western Users of SAS and Open-Source Software]

In September 2024, I had the incredible opportunity to attend WUSS 2024 (Western Users of SAS and Open-Source Software)
in Sacramento, California. The conference brought together experts and enthusiasts from various fields to explore innovative approaches to data analysis, focusing on both proprietary tools like SAS and open-source solutions. I had the privilege of presenting my machine learning project, "Flight Delay Analysis and Prediction", where I demonstrated predictive techniques using open-source data and machine learning algorithms. The project received positive feedback, reinforcing the importance of using data to tackle real-world problems.
Additionally, I was honored to receive a student scholarship, which not only recognized my academic achievements but also enabled me to participate in hands-on workshops and discussions. These sessions focused on open-source tools such as R and Python, allowing me to deepen my knowledge of how these technologies are driving the future of data analysis. The conference provided a platform to engage with leading professionals, exchange ideas, and learn about cutting-edge techniques in analytics and machine learning.
